How many cylinders are the most popular for gasoline vehicles?

The data explored here is from automobile manufacturers in 2017. In the dataset, only a small percentage of vehicles listed are diesel and electric (4% diesel and 7% electric), majority are gasoline powered. Let's explore which type of gasoline vehicles are most popular assuming this dataset represents the population of vehicles; we can see that 4-cylinder vehicles are the top vehicles. In fact, fuel efficiency has become an important factor in owning a vehicle in today's world. According to USA Today, V6 engines accounted for only 27% of retail sales in 2020, compared with 40% in 2005. In the same time, the four-cylinder engine has gone from 30% to 54%. With the electric vehicle market continuing to grow, it is to be seen when the electric cars take over the gasoline powered vehicles.
