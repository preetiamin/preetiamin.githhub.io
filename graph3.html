The data explored here is from automobile manufacturers in 2017. In the dataset, only a small percentage of vehicles listed are diesel and electric (4% diesel and 7% electric), majority are gasoline powered. Let's explore which type of gasoline vehicles are most popular assuming this dataset represents the population of vehicles; we can see that 4-cylinder vehicles are the top vehicles. In fact, fuel efficiency has become an important factor in owning a vehicle in today's world. With the electric vehicle market continuing to grow, it is to be seen when the electric cars take over the gasoline powered vehicles.
